Corporate Strategy & Governance

Ortogon supports executives, boards of directors, and shareholders during moments of strategic decision-making, transformation and transition, where choices have irreversible consequences.

What we do

Ortogon intervenes when structural decisions become unavoidable, but are not clearly formulated, owned, or resolved.

We support our clients with:

  • Developing a clear-eyed understanding of their situation

    Shedding light on the situation, including what is implicit or uncomfortable: economic constraints, internal balances, power dynamics, and unspoken expectations.

  • Identifying the decisions that truly shape the future

    Eliminating false choices that delay or dilute decision-making, and surfacing the real trade-offs: growth, pace, control, and risk.

  • Resolving decision-making deadlocks and blockages

    Clarifying roles, responsibilities, and decision authority, so choices can be made without avoidance, paralysis, or ambiguous compromise.

  • Owning the strategic and capital consequences

    Where relevant, translating decisions into coherent strategic and financial trajectories: financing paths, capital allocation, shareholder governance, or preparation for a transaction.

We do not produce more analysis.

We enable decisions to be made clearly, at the right moment, and with a shared understanding of their implications.

How We Work

Our role is to reduce complexity, make choices intelligible, and enable sound decisions to be taken.

Execution and transactions are carried out by the client’s teams and their partners, on a robust, shared foundation shaped and adapted to the situation.

Our involvement ends when:

  • Key decisions have been taken
  • Responsibilities are clear
  • Execution can move forward without depending on us
